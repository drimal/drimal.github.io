About Me

Experienced researcher in Experimental Nuclear/Particle Physics. Worked on major physics experiments with international collaborations including MINERvA@ FermiLab and Two-photon exchange experiment at Jefferson lab, VA. Expertise in different stages of particle physics data analysis workflow including data pre- and post-processing, quality assurances, clustering and filtering, signal selection and noise reduction, drawing statistical inferences, and hypothesis testing. Worked on automating data processing and handling pipelines using high performance computing facilities at JeffersonLab, Fermilab and Open Science Grid. Have published papers and notes documenting experimental methodologies, data analysis techniques and experimental results in peer-reviewed journals.
